Want some Mac n that Cheese

Where we're going, you won't need a roux.

Utilizing a helping hand from velvetta or another brand of smooth melting cheese, makes this golden dish perfect for filling that need for a quick and easy last minute side dish.

Ingredients

  • 8oz. Velvetta, or other brand of quick melt cheese (cubed)
  • 16oz. Extra Sharp Chedder (shredded or cubed)
  • 16oz. Sharp Chedder Cheese (shredded or cubed)
  • 16oz. Colby Jack (shredded or cubed)
  • 16oz. Dry pasta of choice
  • 2cups Whole milk
  • (optional) Creole Seasoning
  • (optional) Bread Crumbs

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350o
  2. Cook pasta until al dente following directions on the box
  3. Rinse pasta after draining making to stop them from cooking anymore (save about half a cup of pasta water for later)
  4. In a large pot over medium-high heat combine milk and saved pasta water
  5. Slowly begin to add cheeses making sure to stir frequently depending on the level of heat to avoid burning the cheese to the pot
  6. Do NOT add rougly 4oz each of the sharp and colby jack (if shredded), we will use this to top the dish
  7. Once cheese mixture is melted and smooth add pasta to a large casserole dish or aliuminum tray and mix with your cheese sauce
  8. Sprinkle remaining cheese on top of tray along with optional ingredients
  9. Cover tightly with aliuminum foil and bake for 20mins
  10. (Optional)With 5 mins left remove foil and leave under broiler until cheese is nice and brown
