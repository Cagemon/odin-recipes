German Pancake

The family wanted to travel, I said, "Why not Germany?" and plugged the blender in

An easy to make staple all your children should agree on, and only four ingredients!

Ingredients

  • 6 tps of unsalted butter
  • 9 whole eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups All purpose flour
  • 1 1/2 cups milk
  • (optional) Toppings (Fruits, powerdered sugar, syrup, cool whip)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 400o
  2. While oven is preheating place butter into 9x13 casserole dish inside oven
  3. combine all other ingredients, except the toppings into a blender until a smooth creamy batter is formed
  4. After oven is preheated remove dish with melted butter, spread butter to cover bottom and pour mixture right in
  5. Replace dish back in over and let cook for rougly 25mins, you may finished sooner, or later depending on your particular oven
  6. Remove once more after toothpick in center comes out clean
  7. (Optional)Top with your desired choice of toppings
